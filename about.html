About the school

Government Boys Senior Secondary School, Phase-II, Ashok Vihar is a boys’ government institution in North West Delhi, run by the Directorate of Education, GNCT of Delhi and affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

The school was established around 1974 and has been serving students from classes 6 to 12 for several decades, focusing on academic excellence, discipline and social responsibility.

Vision & mission

Vision: To provide accessible and quality education to all boys in the neighbourhood, enabling them to become confident, responsible and productive citizens.

Mission: To nurture students through a balanced emphasis on academics, co-curricular activities and values, in line with the policies of the Directorate of Education and CBSE guidelines.
