Classes offered

  • Middle section: Classes VI to VIII (boys).
  • Secondary: Classes IX and X, following CBSE curriculum.
  • Senior Secondary: Classes XI and XII with streams as per Delhi Government norms (Science/Commerce/Arts.

Curriculum

The school follows the CBSE curriculum, aligned with the National Education Policy and the guidelines of the Directorate of Education, Delhi.

Students are assessed through periodic tests, internal assessments and annual/board examinations, with emphasis on both scholastic and co-scholastic areas.

Co-curricular activities

Like other Delhi government senior secondary schools, the institution participates in activities such as sports, cultural programmes, NSS and various competitions at zonal and district levels.

Students are encouraged to develop leadership, teamwork and social awareness through these initiatives.
